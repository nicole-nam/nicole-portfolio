Creating from scratch: One of my favourite pastimes is cooking—I love being able to create something beautiful from nothing. Of course, cooking also requires a keen attention to detail, and a lot of patience(imagine me slicing a box of shallots for example). Creator who chooses curiosity over complacency every time: I am curious.
